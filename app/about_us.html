Dress right, feel right, anytime, anywhere

The Showroom is a place for people to discover what to wear for every event, at any place and any time. How many times in the last week did you ask yourself, what should I wear for the party, for school, for work, for the wedding or any other type of event? We understand that finding just the right dressing for A specific event is hard and takes too much time and energy. We believe that no one knows better than the people who are coming to that same event. We decided to make things is easier by combining technology with the personal recommendations from people who are coming to the same event in order to create a personalized experience that is also easy and fun.
